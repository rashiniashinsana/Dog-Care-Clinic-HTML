The Dog Care Clinic in Mihiripanna, Thalpe, Sri Lanka, is a facility dedicated to the health and well-being of dogs in the local community.

It offers a range of services aimed at keeping dogs healthy and happy. These services may include vaccinations, deworming, flea and tick prevention, spaying and neutering, general health check-ups, dental care, and emergency medical treatment. The clinic may also provide grooming services such as bathing, nail trimming, and hair trimming to keep dogs clean and comfortable. Additionally, they might offer behavioral training and advice to help owners better understand and care for their pets.

Overall, the Dog Care Clinic in Mihiripanna serves as a valuable resource for dog owners, ensuring that their furry companions receive the necessary care to lead long and fulfilling lives.
