DAILY TOURS


In order to permanently change and improve the situation of the street dogs in Sri Lanka, we also take care of the animals outside the clinic. What once started with feeding the dogs at the surrounding temples has grown steadily over the years. We now run the most comprehensive feeding program for street dogs on the island. Every single day, on three tours, we feed a total of about 700 dogs and cats that would otherwise go hungry. The first team leaves the clinic in the morning before 8 am. The last team returns just before sunset at 6:30 pm.

In total, we are on the road with three tuktuks and need 450 kilograms of food - every single day. Our daily tours are an important and effective way to create a smaller healthier dog population. All the animals we feed are neutered and vaccinated.

We can immediately identify newly arrived dogs and catch them for neutering. Diseases are detected by us in the early stages and the presence of our teams in the surrounding villages creates awareness and trust. In this way, we also carry out important educational work. Without our feeding tours the animals would be lost. Your donation helps us to stay on the road and to do sustainable animal welfare!
