The DCC 50+ Program - Help for Animals AND People

The DCC 50+ program enables us to reach several generations at the same time since the elderly often live with their grown-up children and grandchildren. Even the little ones learn how nice it is to have a dog as a companion. With a donation or sponsorship for this program you help us to help animals AND people in Sri Lanka!
