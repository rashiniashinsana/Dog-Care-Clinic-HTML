PEDIGREE DOGS

Despite growing awareness of the issue, there remains a lack of effective regulation and enforcement to combat the cruelty of puppy mills. Many of these operations operate under the radar, making it difficult for authorities to monitor and shut them down. Additionally, consumers continue to fuel the demand for pedigree dogs without considering the ethical implications of where these dogs come from. It is imperative that governments enact and enforce stricter laws to regulate dog breeding practices, while also educating the public about the importance of adopting from shelters or reputable breeders who prioritize the health and welfare of their animals. Only through collective action can we hope to put an end to the suffering inflicted upon countless dogs in puppy mills around the world
