All animals deserve a happy life !
Please help us make this a reality !



We have been able to accept farm animals in the Dog Care Clinic since 2021.

We have created a separate area for them and have employees who only look after our ungulates. The cows are led to fresh grass every day and are also given concentrated feed as well as fruit and vegetables.

The animals that come to us are very emaciated, disabled or injured and have to be nursed up for many months.
